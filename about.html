About DreamSpace Interiors

Designing Spaces That Feel Like Home

Based in Manipal, Udupi, we craft elegant and practical interiors that reflect your lifestyle. Every project—whether a cozy apartment, a café, or a modern office—is designed with care, creativity, and local craftsmanship.

Our Story

DreamSpace Interiors was founded in 2015 with one goal — to make professional interior design accessible and personal for homes and businesses in coastal Karnataka.

Over the years, we've completed 200+ residential and commercial projects across Manipal, Udupi, and Mangalore. From compact apartments to boutique hotels, our approach stays consistent — thoughtful design, honest materials, and functional beauty.

Our Journey

Key moments that shaped DreamSpace into the studio it is today.

The Beginning

DreamSpace Interiors was founded in Manipal with a vision to bring thoughtful, personalized design to coastal Karnataka.

2015

First Commercial Project

Completed our first café interior in Udupi, marking our entry into commercial design and establishing local partnerships.

2017

Expanding the Team

Grew our team to include specialized designers and project managers to handle increasing demand across the region.
